Who are The OJM Experience

The OJM Experience is a jazz duo formed at USC in Columbia, SC in 2022. We started the band originally to improving our own skills and making tips on the side to help fund ourselves through college. Eventually we started getting gigs and became regular performers at The Soda City Market in Columbia. We play a variety of genres from jazz standards to pop song covers and on most Saturdays you can find us playing at Soda City from 9am - 1pm.
